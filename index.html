Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science,
University of Bucharest

Associated Researcher,
Institute for Logic and Data Science

I completed in 2024 my PhD in proof mining supervised by Laurențiu Leuștean. I am also interested in type theory and interactive theorem proving, the language I know best being Lean. I am also an associated researcher at the Institute for Logic and Data Science.
